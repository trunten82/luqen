**Change 1 — AI Summary panel: graceful unavailable state**

Currently when `llmEnabled=false`, the AI Summary tab button still appears in the tab bar (line 106-108: `{{#if llmEnabled}}`). However the panel content is also wrapped in `{{#if llmEnabled}}` (line 546) meaning no panel is rendered. This causes a broken UX: the tab button appears but clicking it shows nothing.

Fix: When `llmEnabled=false`, still render the panel div (without hx-get) with an informative message:

Replace the existing AI summary panel block (lines ~546-557) with:
```handlebars
<div class="rpt-tab-panel rpt-tab-panel--hidden" id="panel-ai-summary" role="tabpanel" aria-labelledby="tab-ai-summary"
  {{#if llmEnabled}}
  hx-get="/reports/{{scan.id}}/ai-summary"
  hx-trigger="revealed"
  hx-swap="innerHTML"
  {{/if}}>
  {{#if llmEnabled}}
  <div class="rpt-ai-summary__loading">
    <div class="skeleton" style="height:1rem;margin-bottom:var(--space-sm);"></div>
    <div class="skeleton" style="height:1rem;margin-bottom:var(--space-sm);width:80%;"></div>
    <div class="skeleton" style="height:4rem;"></div>
  </div>
  {{else}}
  <div class="rpt-ai-summary rpt-ai-summary--unavailable">
    <p class="rpt-ai-summary__body">{{t "reportDetail.aiSummaryUnavailable"}}</p>
  </div>
  {{/if}}
</div>
```

Remove the outer `{{#if llmEnabled}}` / `{{/if}}` wrappers that previously excluded the entire panel block — the panel should always render (tab button already conditionally rendered).
